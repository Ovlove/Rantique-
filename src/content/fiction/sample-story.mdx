# The Lantern in the Fog

The fog rolled in with a whisper. Maren gripped the flickering lantern tighter, unsure if the footsteps behind her were real... or just echoes of a memory.

> “Not all who wander are lost,” her grandmother once said. But Maren was beginning to doubt that.

She stepped forward.
